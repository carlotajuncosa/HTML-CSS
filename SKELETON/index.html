To follow the Skeleton Brunch trend on Tik Tok:

The Skeleton Brunch trending topic started within niche TikTok communities such as WitchTok, ArtTikTok, and TikTokArt. It involves the user setting up their table with a special guest - a skeleton. If the skeleton centerpiece is absent from the video at first, the user usually comes into view dressed in a skeleton costume themselves. The craze is a reinvention of the viral Halloween Brunch clips that took over TikTok in October of 2021. The original poster of the trend - as well as the first round of clips that caused the fad to go viral - remains unknown.
